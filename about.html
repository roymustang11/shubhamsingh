Professional Journey

With a rich background in **Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR)** and **Optical Remote Sensing**, my academic and professional career has been driven by a passion for uncovering patterns in environmental data. After completing my **Ph.D. in Physics at IIT (BHU) Varanasi**, I ventured into both industry and academia, gaining hands-on experience in geospatial data analysis.

As a **Postdoctoral Fellow at Tufts University**, I continue to explore advanced geospatial technologies, focusing on practical solutions for **urban planning, environmental monitoring, and sustainable development**. My journey includes collaborations with renowned researchers, publication in high-impact journals, and leadership in innovative projects.
