Hello, I'm Shubham Singh

I am a **Postdoctoral Fellow at Tufts University**, specializing in **Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR)** and **Optical Remote Sensing** technologies. I hold a **Ph.D. from IIT (BHU) Varanasi** and a **BS-MS from IISER Bhopal**.

Previously, I worked as a **Geospatial Analyst at GalaxEye Space, Bengaluru**, developing innovative SAR data analysis pipelines. My expertise bridges academia, industry, and cutting-edge satellite technologies.
